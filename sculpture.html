HEXVETICA SCULPTURE

A logo brought into three dimensions through material, form, and structure.

This project explores how a flat logo can exist as a physical object. Starting from a fruit-based graphic design assignment, the HexVetica mark was developed from organic shapes and then translated into a layered wooden sculpture. The piece connects identity design, sculpture, and space—turning a digital logo into something tactile and dimensional.
